*Spectral Plots That Actually Show the Chemistry*

## YouTube packaging

- **Thumbnail angle:** split screen — the bare default `plt.plot` on the left,
  the annotated, colour-coded, shaded final figure on the right, with
  "SAME DATA" stamped across the middle.
- **First 15 seconds (the hook):** show the truncated-y-axis trick turning
  identical batches into a "failed" batch, then snap back to the honest axis.
  Lead with how easy it is to fool yourself.

absorbance in the 505–535 nm window exceeds the others by more than 3× the noise
level — an automated contaminant check, built on the figure you just learned to
